Frequently Asked Questions

Is SaferTab free?

Yes, completely free. No premium tiers, no hidden fees.

Does SaferTab collect my data?

No. Everything is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage. Nothing is ever sent to a server.

Can my child disable SaferTab?

Not without your parent password. The settings panel is locked behind a password gate, and the extension can only be removed through Chrome's extension manager.

How does the adult content filter work?

SaferTab maintains a curated list of known explicit domains and URL patterns. Any matching site is blocked before it loads.

Does it work on all websites?

SaferTab works on all standard HTTP and HTTPS websites. Internal Chrome pages (like settings or the new tab page) are not affected.
